Largest Pool of Human Resource

Omniversity aggregates the largest pool of human resource and supports them with training and first of its kind competency management system. With this second pillar OmniversityMahacharyasourabh J sarkar laid the foundation of an integrated human resource development system for people across sectors.

Virtual Learning Space

Omniversity also functions as a virtual learning space into which people register. What's unique about the Omnivarsity is that unlike Institution based Human Resource Department, here the learners will not be required to switch systems as they move through their career.

Pan- India Implementation

From the farmer of Orissa, the youth of Rajasthan, the drivers of Bengal, Health workers of Bengal, Vocational trainees of Maharastra, Omniversity is being tested across India currently, with diverse socio economic groups.
