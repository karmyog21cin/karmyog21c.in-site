Vocational-skill Programs

KarmYog Institute of Life-skills & Micro-entrepreneurship

Operates on an apprenticeship based earn-while you earn model and Integrates life-skills and micro-entrepreneurship programs with core vocational skills. Vocational academies under this institute are: Academy of Horticulture & Landscaping, Academy of Hospitality & Facility Management & Academy of Learning Experience Design & Development & Academy of Holistic Health. Supported by HIDCO,TATA TRUSTS, NSDC & PMKVY.

System of Rice Intensification (SRI)

A mass education program for farmers educating them on the SRI method of rice cultivation which guaranteed more yield with less production cost. Supported by TATA TRUSTS as an initiative to solve the problem of food security.

Academic Skill Programs

Gunakul

Gunakul is a residential school for the exceptionally talented children, where a group of shishyas (learners) live with their Guru (teacher or guide), and learn & grow under his or her direct tutelage. They learn not only through formal sessions of learning, but by sharing a life and lifestyle that is integrated with the life and lifestyle of the Guru or Guide.
Nested in the KarmYog Ashram, which is home to Gunagrahis, Gunacharyas and a variety of people who come there to be a part of the KarmYog for 21st Century movement founded and led by Mahacharya Shri sourabh J. sarkar.
