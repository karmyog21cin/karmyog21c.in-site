Karmyog For 21 st Century is a movement propelled by visionary educationist shri sourabh j. sarkar that strivesto melt the growing wall between the professional and the personal spheres of life. A wall that detaches our sense of ‘being’, into roles of ‘doing’ and violently fractures the sense of wholesomeness. Ky21c uses work life as a path for personal growth …and personal growth as a path for developing work life by combining the best of the ancient with that of the modern. It draws upon the eternal values of Karm-Yog and applies it into the context of our 21st century work-life.Ky21c functions through three of its essentially diverse yet powerfully adjoined pillars-
KarmYog Foundation,
KarmYog for 21 st
Karmyog Education Network
which combines to create the KarmYog Ecosystem. At the heart of the movement is a focus on Life- skill that would bring about positive transformations in the way education addresses the process of human development and ultimately democratize education for all.

KarmYog Foundation is a charitable institution dedicated to the personal and professional development of Mankind. The Foundation is inspired by the principles of KarmYog for the 21st Century or KY21C. KarmYog Foundation is the tool through which ky21c reaches out to those living in the fringes of every section of society and ushers them into the realm of inclusive growth.One of the primal goal of the foundation is to democratise education for one and all. For the last 25 years, the foundation has been involved in imparting mass literacy across various sectors and underprivileged target groups. KarmYog Education Network is an ecosystem of Focused Learning Companies founded by visionary educationist and life skill expert MahacharyaShri sourabh J sarkar and Los Angeles based renowned child psychologist Dr.Chuck Schreiner. In a venture like this, the stakeholders are able to include yet transcend their personal interests, by organizing, creating and managing a business that solves a social problem- ‘How to make education affordable for one and all?.Here profit is uniquely seen as an essential enabler for running and growing the business, and not as a means for the owners to garner personal wealth. The primary goal of KarmYog Education Network is to contribute to the process of manifesting the good, the true and the beautiful.

Our Business Model

“KarmYog for 21st Century is a movement dedicated to making quality education affordable for all. This is done through its three-pillared business model – OmniDEL TM , OmniVarsityTM and OmniMartTM. While OmniDELTM ensures quality education distributed through OmniVarsityTM, OmniMart TM works towards making it affordable. OmniMart TM creates a revenue stream based on the products and services that emerge largely through the learning and development process; and when these products and services are offered to the market, they actually bring in value that helps to subsidize the cost of providing quality education and development. This makes quality learning affordable and and in the process truly democratizes education itself. Now this of course is the vision... and we have started moving steadfast towards realizing this dream at
KarmYog Green Village”.
Shri sourabh J. sarkar
Param Sevak, KarmYog Foundation
